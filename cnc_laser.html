Description

A side-project I've been thinking about since middle school. A two-axis laser plotter capable of engraving wood.

Hardware used:

-makerbase mks dlc 2.1

-nema17 stepper motors

-4W LaserTree laser module

-RPI 3A+ (cncjs server)

Firmware used:

-GRBL 1.1 (opensource, GitHub)

Software used:

-CNCjs (opensource, GitHub)

-InkScape lasertools extension (GitHub)
