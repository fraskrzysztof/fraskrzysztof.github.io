Description

Arduino micro based, 3d printed flight sim throttle controller. Keypad pattern is optimized for Elite Dangerous gameplay and represent following binds:


1 2 3 4

q w e

a s d

z x space

Thumb buttons on the handle are assigned for hyperdrive and boost. Switch idea is to cut off throttle lever while fuel scooping or auto docking, but there is no such option in game. In fact it overwrite throttle with 0% all the time. It works, but it's not very elegnat solution.



Hardware used:

-Arduino Micro

-used mechanical keyboards switches

Firmware used:

-ArduinoJoystickLibrary ( GitHub)
